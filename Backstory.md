Main plotline:
In the barren lands of the once prosperous kingdom, wherein ashes cover the landscape, the only voice that can be heard is the howling wind.
This used to be a beautiful and comfortable paradise. 
A wild horde of barbarians swept the land dry, burning and pillaging everything in their path.
The only proof of the kingdom's existence is the broken and shattered castles that still crumble to this day, overrun with nature and beasts.
For the citizens of this once great kingdom all hope seems lost.
Scattered by disease and famine, their numbers dwindle.
There is a rumor. A rumor you've heard from the elders.
In the deepest and darkest dungeon, theere is an artifact.
You are their only hope to restore this cursed land back to its' former glory.
Find the artifact and rebuild the kinghdom, reunite the shattered army.
